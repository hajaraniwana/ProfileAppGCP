Short Story About Me

I am Hajarani Syadwana, but you can call me Wana. I come from Simalungun, North Sumatra. My passion lies in the fields of Science (IPA) and Mathematics. I thoroughly enjoy learning about computers because, in my opinion, it's incredibly challenging. I am committed to continuous learning and sharing knowledge with my peers.

Currently, I am in the process of completing my thesis, which focuses on Machine Learning. It has been an enriching journey, and I am excited to contribute to the academic community with my research findings.

Throughout my educational journey, I have been fortunate to have wonderful teachers and mentors who have inspired me. Their guidance has fueled my passion for learning, and I am grateful for their support.

As I progress in my academic and personal endeavors, I look forward to new challenges and opportunities that come my way. I believe that continuous growth and the willingness to embrace change are essential in today's dynamic world.
